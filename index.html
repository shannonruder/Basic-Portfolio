About Me

I'm an online independent media specialist who has worn many hats over the years; as creative director, graphic designer, project manager, illustrator, presentation/kiosk designer, video editor, animator and webmaster. My first major client was Verizon Wireless Interactive Demo Center where I assisted designing and delivering educational, retail and government demo applications for Verizon's intelligent home in Cascades, Virginia. Other clients include The Washington Post, Ernst & Young Consulting, and USAID.

 

I currently live in Altadena, California with my husband, two daughters and a goofball retriever named Rilee.
